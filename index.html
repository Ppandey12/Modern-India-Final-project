1991 Economic Liberalization: The Reform That Saved India

Indias 1991 economic liberalization marked a significant turning point in the countries economic policies. This crisis was centered in the countries payment deficit which was caused by over reliance on imports. Under the leadership of Finance Minister Dr. Manmohan Singh, the Indian government undertook a series of reforms aimed at opening up the economy to global markets and reducing state intervention.
